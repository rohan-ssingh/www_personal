Bio

I am majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Religion at Columbia Engineering. I am an Egleston Scholar, awarded to the top 1% of Columbia Engineering applicants (Link).

My research interests lie at the intersection of artificial intelligence, software systems, healthcare technologies, and cybersecurity, with a focus on developing innovative solutions such as AI-powered medical devices, robust chatbot evaluation tools, and privacy-enhancing browser extensions.

My past research experiences have involved artificial intelligence, software systems, cybersecurity, and healthcare technologies. I have conducted research at:

  • the Systems Software lab at Columbia, under Prof. Junfeng Yang and Wei Hao;
  • the SEEDS Cybersecurity Center at the University of Arkansas under Prof. Qinghua Li;
  • the Thomson Lab, at Caltech under Surya Hari;
  • the AI Campus at Arkansas State University, under Prof. Jason Causey;
I interned at Walmart Global Tech as a Software Engineering and Computer Vision intern.
